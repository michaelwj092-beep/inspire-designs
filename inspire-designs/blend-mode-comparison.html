Characteristics

Overlay

  • Balanced blend
  • Preserves both texture and color
  • Medium contrast
  • Best for: Subtle, professional look

Multiply

  • Darkens the result
  • Emphasizes texture shadows
  • More dramatic and grungy
  • Best for: Bold, industrial aesthetic

Hard-Light

  • High contrast effect
  • Brightens lights, darkens darks
  • Very dramatic
  • Best for: Maximum texture visibility
